About Tim

I'm passionate about science and technology. I believe in the power of technology to solve difficult problems and am an advocate of data driven decision making. I find biology endlessly fascinating and believe in the importance of biomedical research. For these reasons I earned a PhD in molecular biology from the University of Pennsylvania. I later fell in love with computer science and am now working on a MS in Computer Science from Northeastern University at their new Seattle branch campus. I am particuarly interested in data analysis and machine learning and I'm especially interested in how these may aid scientific research, improve decision making, and help find interesting/actionable information burried in a barage of data.

The motivation for this site is threefold. First I wanted to start a blog and needed a place to host it. Secondly, most of the work I have done would be considered "back-end" and I wanted to familarize myself with "front-end" technologies. Thirdly, I wanted a place to showcase my work for potential employers. All the designers I know have their own site, why can't us developers have them as well.

While I will be gradually improving the look of the site, I'm not a designer and appearance isn't the primary goal. If it where I would have used a service. Instead I built something from scratch because that's more fun.
