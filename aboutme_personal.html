About Tim

I'm passionate about science and technology. I believe in the power of technology to solve difficult problems and am an advocate of data driven decision making. I find biology endlessly fascinating and believe in the importance of biomedical research. For these reasons I earned a PhD in molecular biology from the University of Pennsylvania. I later fell in love with computer science and am now working on a MS in Computer Science from Northeastern University at their new Seattle branch campus. I am particuarly interested in data analysis and machine learning with particular interests in how these may aid scientific research, improve decision making, and help find interesting/actionable information burried in a barage of irrelevant data.
